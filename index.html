About 关于我

  • I obtained my PhD degree from Universidad Politécnica de Madrid in 2016. I was a visiting scholar at the University of South Carolina.
  • I have granted NSFC-General Program and NFSC-Youth Program (Class C), and selected into Shanghai Oriental Talents Program.
  • My Group recruits master's and doctoral students year-round. Students majoring in EE & CS and related disciplines are welcome to consult and apply.

  • 2016年博士毕业于Universidad Politécnica de Madrid（国家公派），University of South Carolina访问学者。
  • 主持国家自然科学基金面上项目、青年基金，入选上海市东方英才计划、江苏省双创人才、长三角G60科创走廊突出贡献人才团队。
  • 获中纺联教学成果奖二等奖（排2、排3）、UCIC-SER Young Scientist Award、TI杯/蓝桥杯/大唐杯优秀指导教师奖等。
  • 团队常年招收硕士和博士研究生，欢迎电子信息和计算机大类的同学咨询和报考，有机会推荐至国外一流大学交流和学习！
